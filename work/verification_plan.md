- Hedrick hot V60 — **VERIFIED.** Parameters correct (20g/340g/1:17/100°C/50+50+120+120/~3:00). **NAME WRONG:** "Double Bloom" belongs to Standout Coffee (Lukas/@nordkaffeeguy). Hedrick calls his "Ultimate Pourover Recipe" or "V60 Brew: A Versatile Recipe for Any Coffee."
